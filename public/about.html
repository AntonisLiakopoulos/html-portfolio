About Me

Antonis Liakopoulos is an award-winning screenwriter, script consultant, and copywriter based in Athens, Greece, with a professional background in Marketing and Graphic Design. He has been employed in the domestic film and TV industry. Most important accolades: International Page Awards Semi-finalist, Scriptapalooza Semi-Finalist and International Screenwriters Association Fast-Track Top 50 unproduced writers in 2017. He has a B.A. with a major in Graphic Design and Visual Communication and an M.A. with a major in Screenwriting.
